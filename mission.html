Our Mission is to

Close The Gap

We believe functionality is a human right, not a luxury. We're setting a new stage for a more inclusive future in prosthetic technology.

Our "Why"

Prosthetic technology has the power to change lives, restoring functionality and independence. Yet, this promise remains unfulfilled for millions. The prohibitively high costs, often exceeding $50,000, combined with the complexity of surgical integration, make advanced prosthetics unreachable for the vast majority, especially in low-resource settings.

We saw this problem firsthand in Bangladesh. We saw the gap between transformative technology and the people who needed it most.

SoulEcho was born from a single question: Can we deliver an effective, affordable, and practical prosthetic solution without compromising on usability or performance? Our $500 AI-powered arm is the answer. We are not just building a product; we are building a more inclusive and accessible future.
