Real Lives,

Real Impact

Our 95% positive response rate comes from real patient trials and feedback.

Salman Ahmed, 17

Student, Bangladesh

Salman, a young student, showed incredible adaptability from childhood. In partnership with VARD, we provided him with a customized SoulEcho arm.

"It has improved my quality of life. I am now able to perform tasks that were previously challenging... it has improved my practical abilities and boosted my confidence."

Halima Khatun, 32

Factory Worker, Dhaka

Halima received a massive injury when her right hand was caught in a machine at her factory, leading to amputation at the wrist.

As a relative of one of our team members, our response team immediately designed a customized prosthetic arm to support her wrist and help her regain independence.

Abu Talha, 25

Former Laborer, Bangladesh

Abu lost his arm in a chainsaw accident. He was one of our early testers, and his feedback was crucial.

In the primary phase, he reported that the "fabrical pads" were uncomfortable, created itching, and caused signal glitches. This direct feedback led us to upgrade to modern electropads, which solved the issues and earned his positive response.

Rahim Uddin, 41

Construction Worker, Sylhet

Rahim sustained a severe crushing injury from a falling steel beam at a construction site, leading to extensive muscle and nerve damage.

Despite multiple surgical attempts, necrosis and infection required a surgical amputation below the elbow. He was fitted with a SoulEcho arm to help restore function.
