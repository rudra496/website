A Revolution in Affordable Bionics

Dual-Sensor Fusion (EMG + SMG)

We combine EMG (muscle electricity) with SMG (ultrasound muscle shape) for a control system that is highly accurate, robust, and less prone to interference from sweat or displacement.

Spring-Assisted Grip

Our novel spring mechanism provides a strong, constant grip without continuous motor power. The motor is only used to *open* the hand, reducing battery consumption by nearly 90%.

Real-Time Haptic Feedback

Embedded vibration motors provide real-time sensory feedback for grip strength. This simulates the feel of touch, making the arm feel like a natural extension of the body.
